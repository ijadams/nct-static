Serving the Defense
Community since 1987.

NCT was established as a Woman Owned Small Business in 1987 to support the Defense Intelligence community.

From our humble beginnings we began to learn everything there is to know about defense contracting technology. Starting with the most basic networks supporting everything from phased array radar to satellite image analysis, we have become one of the oldest companies designing and building solutions supporting our cyber intelligence community.
