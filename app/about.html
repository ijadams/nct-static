About

Serving the Defense
Community since 1987.

Since our humble beginnings in 1987, Network Communications Technology, Inc. (NCT) has developed a renowned expertise and a breadth of experience in design and integration of defense contracting technology and logistical procurement services.

Our team has delivered state-of-the-art solutions for many clients, including federal and local government agencies, prime government contractors, and private sector companies. As a client, you can count on us to plan all aspects of your IT projects, provide a single, knowledgeable point of contact for project management and execution, and guarantee all final deliverables will be met on time and within budget. NCT is happy to work with our clients’ existing equipment and software, if adequate, or advise the procurement of any necessary upgrades.

Our approach effectively takes into account any requirements needed to accommodate growth and expansion to anticipate for the technological changes a company must undergo in one, three, or five years. The approach also serves to achieve accurate staffing, scheduling, and budgeting. Further, it permits all project personnel to focus on the direct objectives of the specific task to which they are assigned. Team members are fully integrated into the overall work effort and are assigned work requirements according to their technical capabilities, experience levels, and their potential to contribute significantly to project goals.

The NCT “Converged Technologies Integration”approach is characterized by the following:

  • Project planning, scheduling, and analysis to define subtasks, determine workloads, and identify potential risks
  • An evaluation of any requirements needed to accommodate growth and expansion so as to anticipate the technological changes that a company
  • A qualified team specifically selected to meet your project requirements
  • Continuous review of progress to establish and maintain budget
  • Quality assurance and configuration management process to ensure the delivery of refined work products
